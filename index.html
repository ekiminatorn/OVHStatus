Unofficial OVH Status Twitter bot.

All incident information displayed at https://twitter.com/ovh_status is fetched from http://status.ovh.com/, the english version of http://travaux.ovh.com/.

If you want real-time incident updates, http://travaux.ovh.com/ is much better, as http://status.ovh.com/ version is slower due to OVH Staff having to translate the incident reports from http://travaux.ovh.com/
